Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

CreatorPulse (“we”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect data you provide (goals, preferences) and data from connected platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Spotify) when you authorize access. This includes metrics like revenue, engagement, and analytics provided by those platforms.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data to power the CreatorPulse dashboard: displaying progress, simulating ads, and showing platform insights. We do not sell your data.

3. Storage & Security

Data is stored using Supabase and other service providers. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase, Stripe (for payments), and platform APIs (TikTok, YouTube, Spotify). Their privacy policies apply to their handling of data.

5. Your Rights

You can disconnect platforms, delete your account, or request access to your data. Contact us for such requests.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session and preferences. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies.
